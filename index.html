About FindIt

Our mission to help students recover lost belongings

Problem Explanation

Students frequently lose their belongings in school, such as textbooks, water bottles, jackets, and electronic devices. Traditionally, there has been no organized digital system to track and recover these items, leading to permanent loss and wasted resources.

Purpose of the System

FindIt was created to provide a professional digital lost-and-found solution specifically designed for school environments. Our platform bridges the gap between lost items and their owners through a centralized, searchable database accessible to the entire school community.

Target Users

Students

All grade levels from 6 to 12

Teachers

School faculty and staff

Administration

School office and management

How the System Helps Students

Faster Recovery

Creates a more organized and efficient school environment
